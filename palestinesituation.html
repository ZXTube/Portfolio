The Situation in Palestine

Palestinans are in a dire situation as they're being brutally attacked by the israeli Occupation. Here are a number of statstics below to help you understand what they are currently undergoing, as of the 29th of february 2024;

There are 30,365 martyred Palestinians
There are 74,925 injured Palestinians
There are 2,000,000 displaced Palestinians
There are 11,270 detained Palestinians
There are 355,000 damaged housing units

The numbers signficantly increase every day. These figures only show the genocidal acts done by israel in Palestine since October 7th 2023, it has been going on for much longer. Those who don't add to the numbers, live in despair watching their loved ones suffer.
